Sign up for PPGA Tabling

Starting in Fall 2021, PPGA tables weekly on Friday afternoons to hand out free condoms, dental dams, lube, safe sex information, and PP swag to students on Ho Plaza in front of WSH! In 2020, during the pandemic, PPGA was able to safely deliver free condoms and dental dams to members of the Cornell community through a google form sign up sheet.

Tabling Sign-Up Sheet

Free Condom Friday tabling has many purposes:

  • We seek to destigmatize sex and sexual health, promote safe sex practices and general reproductive health information, and provide resources for all the above
  • Tabling is for dispensing external/male condoms, dental dams, female condoms, and informational pamphlets on topics such as, but not limited, to birth control, STIs, HPV vaccine, how to donate to abortion funds.
  • Raise awareness about social, political, and/or health issues and how to help Cornell, Ithaca, NY state, nationwide.
  • Tabling is a branding opportunity for both PPGA at Cornell and Planned Parenthood as a larger organization which advocates socio-politically for reproductive rights for all.

Tabling info for PPGA members

  • Any e-board member or g-body member may and is welcomed to sign up for a time slot for Free Condom Friday tabling (purely voluntary action).
  • If you sign up for a slot one day you are not committed to returning for that time slot.
  • An e-board member usually sets up the table and takes it down (our resources are stored in the WRC downstairs in WSH)
